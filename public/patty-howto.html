The daily moves
1

Find anything fast

Press ⌘K / Ctrl K anywhere, or use the top search bar. Jump straight to any client, person, or deal.

2

Open a client

Companies › click a name. You get the profile (units, revenue, competitors), the full activity timeline, contacts, emails, documents, and engagements.

3

Log what happened

On any client, use Add a note for calls, takeaways, and next steps. It lands in the timeline instantly and shows up on Home.

4

Move a deal

On the Pipeline board, use the stage buttons: Intro › Pitch › No-fee › Fee › Won / Lost. Syncs to ClickUp

5

Catch new leads

Website Contact Us submissions auto-create a prospect and a follow-up task. Find them in the Leads inbox (sidebar badge) and hit Mark reviewed.

6

Don't lose old clients

Home flags any past client untouched for 6+ months. The re-engagement banner tells you exactly who to reach out to.

7

Email & documents

Emails and Documents pull live from Outlook, OneDrive, and SharePoint the moment you sign in with Microsoft. No forwarding, no duplicates.

8

Tasks & ClickUp

On Tasks, move a task's status. With ClickUp connected, status changes and comments push back to your ClickUp board automatically.

9

Scope by year

Use the All / 2025 / 2026 / 2027 pills at the top to focus dashboards, pipeline, and lists on a single year.

Who can do what
AdminEverything, plus manage people and connections.
MemberWork the data: notes, pipeline, tasks, leads.
ContributorAdd notes and handle leads.
ObserverView only. No edits.

Admins assign roles on the Access page. New sign-ins start as Observer.
