# PATTY — Product Video Script

**Duration:** 90 seconds | **Tone:** Energetic, cinematic, confident | **Music:** Upbeat, modern, building tension

---

## OPENING (0–8s)

**VISUAL:** Black screen. Orange Service Physics logo appears, zooms center.

**VOICEOVER (energetic, fast-paced):**
"Your client relationships are scattered. Emails here. Documents there. Spreadsheets everywhere."

**SFX:** Quick whooshes, logo lands with a click.

---

## PROBLEM (8–15s)

**VISUAL:** Quick montage of chaos:
- Gmail inbox overflowing (red)
- SharePoint folder tree, deep nested (confusing)
- Slack message thread going nowhere (lost)
- HubSpot pricing page (expensive)
- All images slightly desaturated, fast cuts (1–2 sec each)

**VOICEOVER:**
"No single place for a client. No pipeline visibility. No way to know what's happening."

**SFX:** Discordant tones, rising tension.

---

## REVEAL (15–18s)

**VISUAL:** Black screen. Single word appears in orange, bold, centered:

**PATTY**

**VOICEOVER (calm, confident drop):**
"Meet Patty."

**SFX:** Deep orchestral swell, dramatic pause.

---

## FEATURES MONTAGE (18–70s)

### FEATURE 1: UNIFIED HUB (18–28s)

**VISUAL:** Dashboard loads, full-color, crisp. Hero view of:
- KPI cards (Active, Pipeline Value, Open Tasks)
- Recent activity timeline
- Pipeline summary chart

**VOICEOVER:**
"One hub. All your clients. Real data, live dashboards."

**CAMERA:** Slow zoom into dashboard, cards pulse subtly.

---

### FEATURE 2: RICH CLIENT PROFILES (28–38s)

**VISUAL:** Navigate to a company card (e.g., Cava). Profile expands:
- Industry, founded, HQ, units, employees, revenue, competitors
- Live from ClickUp. Autocompleted from your own data.

**VOICEOVER:**
"Rich company profiles. Not guesswork. Real intel from your ClickUp pages. Founder stories. Revenue. Competitors. Everything in one view."

**CAMERA:** Smooth pan across profile fields, each one highlights in orange as mentioned.

---

### FEATURE 3: PIPELINE REALIGNMENT (38–48s)

**VISUAL:** Switch to Pipeline page. Six-column kanban:
- Intro | Pitch | No-Fee Proposal | Fee Proposal | Won | Lost
- Cards flow smoothly across columns

**VOICEOVER:**
"Your funnel, your way. Intro to pitch to proposal to won. Real stages. Real deals. Real visibility."

**CAMERA:** Drag a card across stages (slow motion), it animates. Column totals update. Orange glow on the winning column.

---

### FEATURE 4: MICROSOFT INTEGRATION (48–58s)

**VISUAL:** Split screen:
- Left: Outlook inbox (live emails flowing in)
- Right: Patty Emails page, emails syncing in real-time

**VOICEOVER:**
"Live Outlook sync. Every email lands in Patty the moment it arrives. No more forwarding. No more gaps."

**SFX:** Subtle notification sound, satisfying.

---

### FEATURE 5: DOCUMENTS + SHAREPOINT (58–68s)

**VISUAL:** Documents page loads. Two sections:
- Live from SharePoint (real-time file listing)
- Live from OneDrive (growing list)

**VOICEOVER:**
"Your proposals, decks, and reports. Live from SharePoint and OneDrive. Linked, not duplicated. Always fresh."

**CAMERA:** Scroll through document rows, each one clickable, links glow orange on hover.

---

### FEATURE 6: AUTOMATION (68–75s)

**VISUAL:** Website form submission → instant prospectification:
- Form fills (name, email, company, message)
- Submit button clicks
- Patty instantly creates: company profile, contact, follow-up task, activity log
- All in under 2 seconds

**VOICEOVER:**
"Website lead? Instant prospect. Task assigned. Team alerted. No manual work."

**SFX:** Satisfying cascade of notifications, quick ding.

---

### FEATURE 7: TEAM COLLABORATION (75–82s)

**VISUAL:** Settings → Connections screen. Readiness ring glows up from 0/4 to 4/4:
- Microsoft 365 ✓ Connected
- ClickUp ✓ Connected
- SharePoint ✓ Connected
- Meeting Notes ✓ Connected

**VOICEOVER:**
"Bring your whole team. Role-based access. Admins, members, contributors, observers. One click to connect Microsoft, ClickUp, SharePoint. Go live."

**CAMERA:** Readiness ring fills with orange, each integration lights up.

---

## CLOSING (82–90s)

**VISUAL:** Full dashboard, sunlit, confident. Zoom out to see the whole interface.

**VOICEOVER:**
"Patty. Your client hub. Built for consultancies. Zero HubSpot. 100% yours."

**TEXT ON SCREEN (fade in, orange):**
github.com/TimWCash/patty

**VOICEOVER (final, warm):**
"Let's go live."

**SFX:** Final orchestral note, resolves to silence.

**VISUAL:** Service Physics + Patty logo side by side, fade to black.

## PRODUCTION NOTES

### Colors
- **Primary:** Service Physics orange (#CF5C36) — use for highlights, accents, CTAs
- **Secondary:** Navy (#022B3B) — UI backgrounds
- **Accent:** Dark teal (#124559) — text, headers
- **Neutral:** White (#F9F8F8) — clean, minimal

### Music Suggestions
- **Intro (0–15s):** Tension building. Synth, rising strings. Modern, tech-forward.
- **Features (18–75s):** Upbeat, energetic. Drums, arpeggios. Confident.
- **Closing (75–90s):** Resolution. Full orchestral swell, lands on a solid note.

### Voiceover Tone
- **Fast, confident, warm.** Not robotic. Not corporate.
- Slight smile in the voice. Conviction, not pitch.
- Pacing: Slightly faster than normal speech (builds energy).

### Screen Recording Tips
- **Resolution:** 1440p minimum, 60fps
- **Cursor:** Hide or fade to orange accent
- **Animations:** Moderate easing (not too slow, not instant)
- **Zoom/Pan:** Slow, smooth, cinematic
- **Colors:** Ensure Patty's real UI matches brand colors

### Export Settings
- **Format:** MP4 (H.264)
- **Codec:** AAC audio, 128kbps
- **Resolution:** 1080p or 1440p
- **Aspect ratio:** 16:9 (landscape)
- **File size target:** <50MB for social sharing

## SCRIPT ALTERNATIVES (If you want variations)

### Shorter Version (45s)
Cut Features 6 (Automation) and 7 (Team Collaboration). Jump from Documents straight to Closing.

### Longer Version (2min)
Add separate scenes for:
- Re-engagement alerts (past clients flagged)
- Year filtering (scope by 2025/2026/2027)
- Activity timeline deep-dive
- RBAC demo (switching user roles)

### B2B/Sales Pitch Version
Emphasize ROI: "Reduce onboarding time by 60%. No more lost emails. Centralized deal pipeline."
