# Why We Built Patty

## The Problem

Service Physics is a strategy consultancy. We sell insights, not widgets. Our work lives in client relationships: the intro call where we understand the problem, the pitch where we prove we can solve it, the proposal negotiations, the implementation, the follow-up for the next engagement.

But our client relationships were shattered across five different tools.

Emails in Outlook. Files in SharePoint and OneDrive. ClickUp tasks that didn't sync with anything. Notes in Slack threads that disappeared. No single source of truth. No way to ask: "What's our total pipeline?" or "When did we last touch this client?" or "Who owns this relationship?"

We'd close a deal, then six months later, a team member would ask, "Wait, what was the outcome on Cava?" and someone would have to dig through email archives.

It was broken.

## The HubSpot Detour

So we looked at HubSpot. Industry standard, right?

HubSpot is built for companies that sell many small things: SaaS, e-commerce, lead magnets. It optimizes for volume: collect leads, nurture them in campaigns, convert them in bulk. It's a sales factory.

But we're not a factory. We sell one engagement at a time. Deep, custom work. A relationship with Cava isn't a "lead" to nurture — it's a partner we'll work with for years. The value isn't in the conversion funnel; it's in the relationship depth.

HubSpot wanted us to pay $10k/year for features we didn't need, in a way that didn't match how we actually work. It felt like wearing someone else's suit.

## The Decision

We decided to build our own.

Not because we wanted to boil the ocean. Not because we wanted to be precious about our workflows. But because the right tool didn't exist, and it was faster and cheaper to build it than to retrofit HubSpot.

We built it for:
- **Consultancies, not factories.** One relationship at a time. Deep, not wide.
- **Our real data.** 85 actual clients from ClickUp. Real revenue, real competitors, real history. Not synthetic sample data.
- **Real integrations.** Live Outlook email. Live SharePoint documents. Live ClickUp tasks. Not sync delays or disconnected islands.
- **Our funnel.** Intro. Pitch. Proposal. Won. Lost. Not "lead → opportunity → closed."
- **Our team.** Roles and permissions that match how we actually work, not corporate org charts.
- **Minimal.** What we need. Nothing we don't.

## The Name

We named it after Patagonia.

Not because we're outdoorsy. But because Patagonia built a company that refuses to sell you what you don't need. They could make more money with more product lines, flashier marketing, bigger ambitions. Instead, they chose clarity: make it good, make it last, sell only what solves the real problem.

That's what Patty is. One tool. Built right. For one kind of business: consultancies that care about relationships more than volume.

## What It Does

Patty is your client hub. All 97 companies. All the context. Real-time email, documents, pipeline visibility, team collaboration. No missing history. No scattered notes.

Sign in once with Microsoft. Everything else flows in: email, documents, team access. Give it your ClickUp token. Tasks stay in sync. Give it your SharePoint site. Documents stay current.

It's not trying to be HubSpot. It's not trying to be Salesforce. It's trying to be the one thing every consultancy needs and can't find: a place where all the client context lives, so you can actually see the business you're building.

## Who It's For

Consultancies with 5-50 people. Deep client relationships. Work that lives in email, proposals, meetings, follow-ups. Teams that use Microsoft 365 and ClickUp already. Teams that want control, not feature bloat.

If you're running a sales factory, Salesforce exists. If you're managing a portfolio of relationships and you need to see them all in one place, Patty is for you.

## The Future

Right now, Patty is live at `localhost:3100`. We're about to deploy it to Azure so the team can use it from anywhere.

Next: live Outlook sync so every email lands in Patty automatically. Then ClickUp two-way sync so task updates flow both ways. Then the team's meeting notes (Zoom AI, Granola, Otter) flow in automatically.

By the end of the summer, Patty will be the single source of truth for every client relationship we have.
